Stop immediately and do not submit if any of these appear:

- Login required and owner is not present.
- CAPTCHA / Cloudflare / Turnstile / security challenge.
- Payment, review fee, sponsor package, paid launch, advertising package, or checkout.
- Reciprocal backlink, badge embed, homepage/footer link, or site modification.
- Request for fake review, voting, endorsement, or undisclosed promotion.
- Form category makes the product misleading.
- The page redirects to a different product/launch flow than expected.
