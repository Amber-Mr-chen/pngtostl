## Competitive Minimum for PRD / QA
- Real PNG upload and validation.
- Real STL generation or a clearly blocked pre-launch state.
- Downloadable `.stl` file or job/download URL.
- File-size and file-type limits stated near upload.
- Privacy/retention language that matches actual storage behavior.
- How-it-works explanation for image quality, height/depth assumptions, and 3D printing expectations.
- Internal expansion route for `image to STL`, but homepage remains `PNG to STL` focused.

## Product Opportunity
- Site type: focused utility-first tool site.
- Initial wedge: exact-match `pngtostl.net`, cleaner single-purpose workflow than broad converter suites.
- Differentiation: explain image-to-STL assumptions clearly, keep upload friction low, avoid generic multi-tool clutter, build trust around file handling.
- Monetization: free v0 first; potential later options are ads, Pro batch/private processing, or affiliate links for 3D printing tools. Pricing must be validated in stage 03.

## Risks
- P0: Launching before real STL output would fail the search intent; keep current site as pre-launch/scaffold until engine is connected or copy is explicitly honest.
- P1: Incumbents have broad format coverage and stronger authority; the site needs better focused UX and topical depth.
- P1: If uploaded files are stored, privacy and retention must be explicit.
- P2: Exact-match domain is narrow; keep `/image-to-stl` as controlled expansion path.
